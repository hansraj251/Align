1. Introduction

Align ("Align", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of the information entrusted to us by Businesses and their authorized users.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect personal information and business information when you access or use the Align website, applications, and related services.

Throughout this Privacy Policy, the term "Business" refers to any restaurant, café, coffee shop, cloud kitchen, bakery, food court, quick service restaurant (QSR), dessert shop, juice bar, catering business, or other food service establishment using the Align platform.

By creating an account, accessing the platform, starting a free trial, purchasing a subscription, or otherwise using Align, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy applies to Business owners, managers, employees, staff members, and any other authorized users who access the Align platform through a Business account.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that is necessary to create, operate, maintain, improve, and support the Align platform. The information collected depends on how the Business and its authorized users interact with our services.

2.1 Business Account Information

When a Business registers for Align, we may collect information such as the Business name, owner's name, mobile number, email address, business address, city, state, GST number, FSSAI number, logo, and other registration details provided during account creation.

2.2 Staff Information

We collect information relating to staff accounts created by the Business, including names, mobile numbers, usernames, roles, login credentials (stored securely), and other information required to manage access to the platform.

2.3 Operational Information

We collect information created while using the platform, including dining areas, tables, menu categories, menu items, quick items, customer orders, kitchen tickets, billing records, reports, and other operational data entered by the Business.

2.4 Payment Information

Subscription payments are processed through authorized third-party payment providers. We may receive payment-related information such as payment status, transaction reference numbers, invoice details, subscription details, and billing history. Align does not store complete payment card or banking information.

2.5 Device and Technical Information

We may automatically collect technical information such as device type, browser type, operating system, IP address, application version, log information, and diagnostic data to maintain platform security, troubleshoot technical issues, and improve service performance.

2.6 Notification Information

Where push notifications are enabled, we may collect notification identifiers, including Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens or similar device identifiers, solely for delivering operational notifications related to the Align platform.

2.7 Support Communications

If you contact us for technical support, feedback, or enquiries, we may collect the information you voluntarily provide, including your name, contact details, messages, attachments, and other information necessary to respond to your request.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only for legitimate business and operational purposes necessary to provide, maintain, improve, and secure the Align platform.

Your information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage Business accounts and staff accounts.
  • To provide and operate the features and services available on the Align platform.
  • To process customer orders, billing records, kitchen operations, reports, and other operational activities initiated by the Business.
  • To manage subscriptions, process payments, generate invoices, and maintain billing records.
  • To send important service-related communications, including account notifications, subscription updates, security alerts, and operational notifications.
  • To provide customer support, respond to enquiries, and resolve technical issues.
  • To monitor platform performance, diagnose technical problems, prevent fraud, detect unauthorized access, and improve the reliability and security of our services.
  • To comply with applicable laws, legal obligations, regulatory requirements, and lawful requests from competent authorities.
  • To develop new features, improve existing functionality, and enhance the overall user experience.

We do not sell your personal information or Business data to third parties.

4. Sharing of Information

Align does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or Business data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances where it is necessary to provide the Align platform or where required by law.

  • Service Providers: We may share limited information with trusted third-party service providers that help us operate the platform, such as payment processors, cloud hosting providers, notification services, analytics providers, and customer support tools.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information where required to comply with applicable laws, legal processes, court orders, government requests, or regulatory obligations.
  • Protection of Rights: We may disclose information where reasonably necessary to protect the rights, property, security, or safety of Align, our users, or other persons, or to investigate fraud, security incidents, or misuse of the platform.
  • Business Transfers: If Align is involved in a merger, acquisition, investment, restructuring, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable legal requirements.

Except for the circumstances described above, we will not share your personal information or Business data with third parties without your consent unless required by applicable law.

5. Payment Information

Subscription payments for the Align platform are processed by authorized third-party payment service providers. Align does not collect or store your complete debit card, credit card, net banking, UPI PIN, CVV, or other sensitive payment credentials.

We may receive and store limited payment-related information, including payment status, transaction reference numbers, subscription details, invoice information, billing history, and other information necessary to manage your subscription and maintain financial records.

All payment transactions are subject to the privacy policies and terms of the respective payment service providers. We encourage you to review their policies before completing any payment.

Align is not responsible for the privacy, security, or data handling practices of third-party payment providers.

6. Data Security

Align implements reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational security measures designed to protect personal information and Business data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, misuse, or destruction.

We regularly monitor and maintain our systems to help ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of the information stored on the Align platform. Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel and is provided only where necessary for operational, support, or legal purposes.

While we take reasonable steps to safeguard your information, no method of transmitting data over the internet or storing electronic information is completely secure. Accordingly, we cannot guarantee absolute security, and you acknowledge that you provide information to us at your own risk.

Businesses are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials, limiting access to authorized users, and promptly notifying Align if they believe their account has been compromised or accessed without authorization.

7. Data Retention

Align retains personal information and Business data only for as long as necessary to provide the platform, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, and operate our services.

During an active subscription or free trial, Business data is retained to support the normal operation of the Align platform.

If a subscription expires and is not renewed, the Business account and its associated data will generally be retained for up to 90 days from the date of subscription expiry. During this period, the Business may renew its subscription and regain access to its retained data, subject to the applicable subscription plan and platform policies.

After the 90-day retention period, Align may permanently delete, anonymize, or otherwise remove the retained data from its systems, unless a longer retention period is required by applicable law or is necessary to protect our legal rights.

Certain records, including billing information, invoices, payment records, or other information required by law, may be retained for a longer period where necessary to comply with applicable legal, tax, accounting, or regulatory obligations.

8. Cookies & Similar Technologies

Align uses cookies and similar technologies to provide, maintain, and improve the functionality, security, and performance of the platform.

These technologies may be used to remember user preferences, maintain secure login sessions, support authentication, improve user experience, analyze platform performance, and help protect against unauthorized access.

Depending on how you access the Align platform, we may also use browser storage technologies, such as local storage or session storage, to securely store information required for the proper operation of certain features.

Most web browsers allow you to control or disable cookies through their settings. However, disabling cookies or similar technologies may affect the availability or functionality of certain features of the Align platform.

9. Third-Party Services

The Align platform relies on certain trusted third-party service providers to deliver, operate, and improve our services. These providers perform specific functions on our behalf and may process limited information only as necessary to provide their respective services.

Third-party services used by Align may include, but are not limited to:

  • Payment service providers for processing subscription payments.
  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers for securely hosting the Align platform and related services.
  • Push notification services for delivering operational notifications to authorized users.
  • Analytics, monitoring, logging, and technical support services used to maintain, improve, and troubleshoot the platform.

Each third-party service provider operates under its own privacy policy, terms, and data handling practices. We encourage you to review their respective policies before using services that involve those providers.

Align is not responsible for the privacy practices, security measures, or content of third-party services that are outside our control.

10. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have certain rights regarding the personal information that we collect and process.

Depending on your relationship with Align and applicable legal requirements, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or updating of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of personal information where retention is no longer necessary or where permitted by applicable law.
  • Request information about how your personal information is processed.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on your consent, subject to applicable legal and contractual obligations.

Business account owners are responsible for managing the information of their authorized staff members within the Align platform. Requests relating to staff accounts may be handled through the respective Business account owner where appropriate.

We may decline or limit certain requests where we are required or permitted to retain information under applicable law, to enforce our legal rights, to prevent fraud, to protect platform security, or to fulfill our contractual obligations.

To exercise any applicable privacy rights, you may contact us using the contact details provided in this Privacy Policy.

11. Children's Privacy

Align is intended solely for use by Businesses and their authorized users. The platform is not directed to, and is not intended for, individuals under the age of 18.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that personal information of a child has been collected without appropriate authorization, we will take reasonable steps to delete such information as required by applicable law.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the Align platform, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.
