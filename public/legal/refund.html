1. Introduction

This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains the terms governing subscription payments, refunds, and cancellations for the Align platform.

Align is a subscription-based software platform provided to Businesses for restaurant and food service management. By purchasing a subscription or using the Align platform, you agree to this Refund & Cancellation Policy.

Throughout this Policy, the term "Business" refers to any restaurant, café, coffee shop, cloud kitchen, bakery, food court, quick service restaurant (QSR), dessert shop, juice bar, catering business, or other food service establishment using the Align platform.

2. Free Trial

Align offers eligible Businesses a one-time free trial period of 30 days to evaluate the platform before purchasing a subscription.

During the free trial, Businesses may access the features and functionality made available under the trial plan, subject to the Terms & Conditions and any applicable platform limitations.

A valid payment method is not required to start the free trial unless otherwise specified by Align.

At the end of the free trial period, continued use of subscription-based features requires the purchase of an active subscription. If no subscription is purchased, access to certain operational features may be restricted in accordance with the Align Terms & Conditions.

Align reserves the right to modify, suspend, limit, or discontinue the free trial offer at any time, or to refuse a free trial where misuse, abuse, fraud, or repeated trial registrations are detected.

3. Subscription Payments

Align is provided as a subscription-based software service. To continue using subscription features after the free trial period, a Business must purchase an active subscription plan.

Subscription fees, billing periods, and available plans are displayed on the Align platform at the time of purchase. By completing a subscription payment, the Business agrees to pay all applicable charges associated with the selected plan.

Subscription payments are processed through authorized third-party payment service providers. Payment confirmation is subject to successful authorization and processing by the applicable payment provider.

Access to paid subscription features will generally be activated after successful payment confirmation. If a payment fails, is declined, or is reversed, Align reserves the right to suspend or deny access to subscription features until payment has been successfully completed.

Businesses are responsible for ensuring that the payment information provided during the purchase process is accurate and that sufficient funds or payment authorization are available.

4. Refund Policy

Align provides digital subscription-based software services. Once a subscription has been successfully purchased and activated, all subscription fees are non-refundable, except where a refund is required under applicable law.

No refunds, whether full or partial, will be provided for unused subscription periods, unused features, temporary inactivity, or early termination of the subscription by the Business.

Businesses are encouraged to make full use of the 30-day free trial to evaluate the Align platform before purchasing a subscription.

Refund requests based on user error, lack of usage, change of business requirements, accidental purchases, or failure to cancel before the subscription period begins will generally not be accepted.

If Align determines that a payment was processed due to a verified technical error caused solely by the platform, we may, at our sole discretion and where appropriate, investigate the matter and provide a suitable resolution in accordance with applicable law.

5. Cancellation Policy

A Business may choose to cancel its subscription at any time. Cancellation will prevent future subscription renewals but will not entitle the Business to a refund of any subscription fees already paid, except where required by applicable law.

Unless otherwise specified, the Business will continue to have access to the subscribed features until the end of the current subscription period. After the subscription expires, access to subscription-based operational features may be restricted in accordance with the Align Terms & Conditions.

Cancellation of a subscription does not automatically delete the Business account or its associated data. Data will be retained and managed in accordance with the Align Privacy Policy and applicable data retention practices.

Businesses that wish to permanently close their account or request deletion of their data may contact Align using the contact details provided in this Policy. Such requests will be processed in accordance with applicable law and the Align Privacy Policy.

6. Exceptions

Nothing in this Refund & Cancellation Policy limits or excludes any rights or remedies that cannot be excluded under applicable law.

Where a refund is required by applicable law, court order, or a binding direction issued by a competent regulatory authority, Align will process the refund in accordance with the applicable legal requirements.

Align reserves the right to investigate refund requests involving suspected fraud, misuse of the platform, unauthorized payment activity, chargeback abuse, or violations of the Align Terms & Conditions. Refunds may be refused where permitted by applicable law.
