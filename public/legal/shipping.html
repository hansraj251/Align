1. Introduction

This Shipping & Delivery Policy explains how subscription-based services are delivered through the Align platform.

Align is a cloud-based software platform. No physical products are sold or shipped. All subscriptions and services are delivered electronically through the platform.

2. Digital Delivery

Align is provided exclusively as a digital software service. No physical goods, printed materials, or hardware are shipped or delivered as part of a subscription unless expressly stated otherwise.

Upon successful registration or subscription activation, eligible Businesses are granted access to the Align platform and its applicable features through their authorized account.

Access to the platform is delivered electronically through the internet. Businesses are responsible for maintaining a compatible device, supported web browser or application, and a stable internet connection to access the services.

3. Subscription Activation

Subscription access is generally activated shortly after successful payment confirmation by the authorized payment service provider.

In certain circumstances, activation may be delayed due to payment verification, technical maintenance, system updates, network interruptions, or other factors beyond Align's reasonable control.

If a Business does not receive access within a reasonable period after successful payment, it should contact Align using the contact details provided in this Policy for assistance.

4. Delivery Delays

While Align aims to provide prompt access to subscription services, temporary delays may occasionally occur due to scheduled maintenance, technical issues, payment verification, internet connectivity problems, or circumstances beyond our reasonable control.

Align will make reasonable efforts to resolve such issues as quickly as practicable. Temporary delays in digital service activation do not automatically entitle a Business to a refund unless required by applicable law.
